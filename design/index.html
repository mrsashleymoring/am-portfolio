Web Design

Stay Hosting Project

Task

Stay Hosting is a property management company that needed a website showcasing all of the services they offer as well as current properties they host. The goal was create a design that is professional, modern, and gets people excited to work with them.

Process

After doing some discovery Q & A with the owners, I built user profiles to better evaluate what colors, typography, and imagery they should use. The primary objective was to have a site that was simple to navigate for the user and guide visitors to connect with Stay Hosting.

Final Products

Stay Hosting wanted a site that guided their visitors to connect with them. They wanted a clean and modern look that also showed they are fun and easy to work with.
