Web Design

Stay Hosting Project

Project Brief

Are you struggling with your online presence? Do you want people to visit your webpage and feel confident in your professional services? Maybe you just want to hire someone to build the website for you so you can focus on your business. That is exactly how I helped Stay Hosting —a property management company based out of Costa Rica— they wanted a website that showcased their services with a design that is professional, modern and gets people excited to work with them.

Process

Working with a team can often be difficult because everyone has their own opinion. The discovery process was extremely important for this project because I needed to dive deep into what each of their priorities were. Each member of the team was given an extensive questionnaire about the business, what their idea of the brand was and who their ideal customers were.
I used all of these answers to build a storyboard which served as a guide to create a website that was simple to navigate for the user and to guide site visitors to connect with Stay Hosting.

Final Products

Having a website that feels natural to your users will increase the amount of time they spend on your site. Every detail is important when it comes to captivating your visitors. By starting the design process with your ideal customer in mind, you increase the likelihood of them connecting with you based simply on how your webpage looks.
