Case Study

Danielle Artigo | Branding

I have a client Danielle, and if you are anything like her, you know what you like and just need a little help getting those thoughts tangible. When Danielle came to me, she had a website, but she didn't feel it 100% represented her brand.

I started by giving Danielle a discovery questionnaire to collect all of the information I needed to understand her brand , her target audience and the overall message she wanted her clients to feel when they got to her webpage.

Danielle already had a logo that she loved, so I used her logo as a base to work from.  One thing that Danielle wanted to avoid was feeling like her website was "woo woo" (her words).  Being in the meditation and wellness industry, there is sometimes a stigma attached that Danielle really wanted to avoid.  

I started by creating some user personas and creating a brand story for Danielle. As a result, I chose blue, gold and purple colors that would inspire trust and warmth with her audience. I used a combination of serif and san serif fonts, because she wanted her brand to be friendly, but also taken seriously.

The Result

In the end, Danielle has a website she loves and connects with her target audience.
