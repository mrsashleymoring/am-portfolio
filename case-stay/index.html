Case Study

Stay Hosting | Custom Welcome Book

Max at Stay Hosting wanted to set apart his services as a property manager for vacation homes in Costa Rica by offering custom welcome books to his clients.  He wanted to be able to offer a personalized, completely custom welcome book for their guests. Max also wanted a way to encourage property guests to book excursions through his website, so he could offer an all in one service.

I started by giving Max a discovery questionnaire to collect all of the content that was needed for the entire booklet. 

I conducted some research about the area and put together some inspiration because I wanted the information to be presented in a fun, bold and easy to skim layout.

I wanted an element that I could use to frame the pages with; I created an elephant leaf icon that I used in different shades of green to layer and position as a frame on each page.  I did not want each page to have the exact same framed look, so I used a repeat of four different positions.​

The Result

Because Max's business is in Costa Rica, and I reside in the United States, I relied heavily on Max's descriptions of the property as well as his answers to our discovery questionnaire in order to bring the project together and achieve the results Max was looking for.

After building a relationship with Max and understanding what exactly he was looking for, the project blew by with ease.  Visitors from all over the world visit Costa Rica and stay in properties like Max's.  We used lots of icons, kept the content very simple and added a QR code in the book that linked back to Max's site for easy bookings and activities.
